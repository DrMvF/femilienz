### femgun

**Dr. Miriam and Jasper von Felbert**  
Gunfluencers. Co-creators. Hunters of resonance.

Born in the North of Germany – rooted in nature and craft.  
Together, we explore the poetic paradox of **Positive Psychology & Precision Shooting**.  
Mental clarity meets technical mastery.

---

**femgun** is a growing initiative to **empower women in hunting**.

We share knowledge, tools, and experiences to strengthen:
- resilience under pressure  
- confidence with firearms  
- and a deep sense of purpose in nature-based leadership.

What began in **Corilienz** now finds a sharper focus:  
A shared rhythm of attention, embodiment, and ethical awareness.
